## What Manus Built

Manus was a **general-purpose AI agent** for businesses:

- Multi-step task execution (files, coding, data analysis)
- B2B subscription model
- Tool-calling orchestration
- Context management for long tasks
- Error recovery (agents failing gracefully)
- Enterprise integrations (Slack, Google, etc.)

### Tech Differentiators
- Not just a chatbot wrapper
- **Multi-step agent** with tools
- Session memory
- Enterprise-grade reliability

## Why Meta Bought It

1. **AI Agent Race**: Meta needs autonomous agents for Meta AI
2. **Enterprise Play**: B2B market is massive ($125M ARR proves demand)
3. **Talent Acquisition**: 100 engineers joining Super Intelligence Lab
4. **Speed**: Build vs buy - Manus had 9 months head start
